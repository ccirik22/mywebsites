ABOUT

AIHAS is a national Turkish brand that has accredited content, is in the accreditation phase of application standards, and measures and reports impulsivity, hyperactivity and attention behaviors in accordance with today's measurement and evaluation standards.

WHY AIHAS

Technological devices have paved the way for happiness on their own. Loneliness has deepened. The healing power of society has diminished. Latent mental problems that were previously compensated by the environment have become apparent. The world has not kept up with this rapid development in terms of impulsivity and hyperactivity. For example, impulsivity has been defined as genetically qualified but not in terms of behavior. As a result, a situation that is defined medically but cannot be dealt with behaviorally has emerged. New scales and new types of interventions have become mandatory. Of course, now, not only the presence or absence of impulsivity, hyperactivity or attention deficit disorder has become important, but also the level at which it is present. AIHAS provides the clearest information on whether attention, impulsivity and hyperactivity disorder exist and also on the quantitative level.

FEATURES

It makes an objective measurement without ignoring the characteristics of Turkish etiology and epidemiological structure. It minimizes the error in measurement by making these measurements, which are made in the world by test method, with a stimulus-response combination. It can measure attention, impulsivity and hyperactivity singly, as well as offering a triple correlational measurement opportunity.

USAGE

  • Suitable for all age groups.
  • Prevents misdiagnosis and unnecessary treatment.
  • It is a product that you can use safely.
  • The study was undertaken by many academics and psychologists who specialize in impulsivity, hyperactivity and attention.
  • It is a maximum of 40 minutes.
  • The application is done online without pen and paper.

REPORTING

A detailed result document is structured in four categories and directed to the school guidance service or school psychologist:

  • Those with normal levels of impulsivity, hyperactivity and attention in the measurement result report
  • Those who need guidance service support in the measurement result report
  • Those who need psychologist support in the measurement result report
  • Those who need psychiatrist support in the measurement result report
