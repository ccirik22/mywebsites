ABOUT US

Our Mission

Dysfunctional modes formed by the combination of schemas that have negative effects on the flow of daily life are quite annoying. This situation creates problems such as anxiety, inadequacy and self-confidence in individuals. The mission of the ic-schema formation is to replace these modes with functional ones.

History

ic-schema is an international organization headquartered in Germany, founded in 1995 together with ICEMDR. In 1998, the academic organization in Europe, France, Sweden, Denmark, Switzerland and Italy was completed. By 2004, protocols were completed with stakeholder institutions in America, Asia and Africa. In 2012, those responsible for the Americas, Europe, Asia and Africa continents were elected. In 2015, the ic-schema application certificate started to be issued in English. The certificate and website were renewed in 2025.
